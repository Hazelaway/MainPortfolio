And one more book (writen by me):

MY OTHER JOURNEY

2001

The first day of university, the head of the Tourism program told us, "You will be apprentices of everything and masters of nothing," almost in a disdainful tone.

2005

I started working in the industry, and I quickly learned and gained a lot of experience.

2010

From Málaga, I went to work in Tenerife. And being more 'isolated,' I started traveling more than ever and continued studying even more.

2020

Global pandemic. Where was I? In Mexico, with a return flight through the U.S. (border closed). What was the epicenter of it all at that time? Madrid. Well, that's where I flew and had a layover (I still don’t know how). After that, about a year of introspection, reconnecting with nature, and new life projects.

2024

Jack of all trades, master of none. I learned and became a certified drone pilot, learned to kitesurf, obtained a C1 level in English, train 3-4 times a week, became an official EU Tourism guide, learned to code, and I’m fascinated by new technologies and the history of Egypt... I'm driven by curiosity and the desire to learn new things.
